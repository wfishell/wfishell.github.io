Welcome!

Hi I am William Fishell, a Master’s student in Computer Science at Columbia University advised by Mark Santolucito, where my work focuses on formal methods, temporal reasoning, and Neurosymbolic AI. I am currently a GRO fellow with the Learning Systems Group at Oak Ridge National Laboratory, developing neuromorphic models that combine spiking neural dynamics for convolution nets. Prior to graduate school, I worked at DraftKings on the Promotional Optimization team, where I led elasticity modeling efforts and helped design and deploy large-scale revenue-optimizing machine learning systems. I hold a Bachelor's from Amherst College were I studied Pure Mathematics and Jazz Composition.
